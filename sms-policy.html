Who Receives SMS Messages?

SMS messages are sent only to authorized internal team members of PILOT PROPERTY INVESTMENT LLC who use the PilotManager platform. This includes property managers, office staff, technicians, operations personnel, and leadership. Messages are operational alerts that support day-to-day property-management work.

We do not currently send SMS to tenants, prospective tenants, leads, or any external consumer audience. If we expand to tenant messaging in the future, tenants will only receive SMS after providing explicit, recorded opt-in consent, and this policy will be updated accordingly before any such messages are sent.

How Internal Users Opt In

A user becomes eligible to receive SMS only after all of the following:

  • An administrator creates a PilotManager account for the team member.
  • The team member signs in to PilotManager and adds their mobile phone number to their user profile.
  • By saving a phone number in their profile, the team member acknowledges this SMS Communication Policy and agrees to receive operational SMS alerts at that number.

The opt-in is per-employee and revocable at any time — see the Opt-Out section below. No phone number receives SMS from PilotManager unless a user has affirmatively saved that number in their own PilotManager profile.

What Messages We Send

All messages are operational alerts about work the recipient is responsible for inside PilotManager. Examples of message types:

  • Daily summary of cash tenants behind on rent
  • Technician daily-report status
  • Reminders about open or due tasks assigned to the user
  • Upcoming eviction-court dates for properties in the user's portfolio
  • Lease-expiration reminders (e.g., 60 days out)
  • Maintenance and inspection alerts

Sample Messages

PilotManager: 5 cash tenants are behind on rent. Open the dashboard to review. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help.
PilotManager: You have 3 tasks due today. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help.
PilotManager: Eviction court tomorrow at 9:00 AM for 1234 Main St. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help.
PilotManager: Lease for 5678 Oak Ave expires in 60 days. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help.
PilotManager: Daily technician report ready — 8 jobs completed, 2 pending. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for help.

Message Frequency

A typical internal user receives approximately 5–10 messages per day during normal business operations, depending on their role and how much activity is open in their portfolio. Frequency is bounded by the events being summarized (tasks, court dates, technician work), not by any marketing schedule.

Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe from all future SMS from PilotManager. You will receive one final confirmation message that your number has been removed.

Reply HELP at any time for assistance.

You may also opt out by removing your phone number from your PilotManager profile, or by emailing heysem4545@pilotpropertyohio.com and asking us to remove your number.

Standard message and data rates may apply. SMS is supported on most U.S. carriers including AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and Sprint. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

PILOT PROPERTY INVESTMENT LLC respects your privacy. Phone numbers used for SMS are collected solely from internal team members who enter their own number into their PilotManager profile. We do not sell, rent, or share phone numbers with third parties. Numbers are used only to deliver the operational alerts described above and are transmitted to our SMS provider (Twilio Inc.) for the sole purpose of message delivery.

When a user opts out (by replying STOP, removing their number, or contacting us), their number is removed from the SMS-eligible list promptly and no further messages are sent.

Questions about how we handle phone numbers, message logs, or any other personal data can be sent to heysem4545@pilotpropertyohio.com.

Future Tenant Messaging

We are evaluating extending SMS to tenants of properties we manage (for example: rent reminders and maintenance-technician ETA notifications). Before any tenant message is sent, we will:

  • Add an explicit tenant opt-in mechanism (signed lease addendum and/or a digital opt-in form on this site).
  • Update this policy with the tenant-specific use case and sample messages.
  • Make the tenant opt-in process publicly visible here.

No tenant will receive SMS without first providing explicit, documented consent.
